Sunny the Sunflower.

← Back to portfolio

What is it?

Sunny is an interactive companion sunflower built using Arduino microcontrollers. Sunny follows the light thanks to photocells mounted on its head. It also reacts when you touch its leaves or petals by retracting them. The more you interact with Sunny, the happier it gets and when the flower reaches its final state of happiness, it will play music and dance for you.

The Process

The process of designing and building Sunny started with testing the hardware to make sure it was capable of moving the flower in a satisfactory way. After the sensors and servo motors were tested, the frame of the flower was attached to them. The code for the flower was then tested to make sure the flower responded in a way that was fun to interact with.

The most difficult part of the process was avoiding breaking the frame of the flower because of the violent movements of the flower. The problem was solved by slowing down the movement of the flower and adding reinforcing parts to the frame.
