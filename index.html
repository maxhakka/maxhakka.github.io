Hi, I'm Max.

I'm a UX Designer based in Stockholm with over 3 years of experience designing and crafting digital products. I help both governmental and commercial organisations achieve their goals by using methods such as user interviews, workshops, user story mapping, prototyping and user testing. I'm also well versed in the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines and have several years worth of experience building and designing responsive and accessible applications.

In most of my projects I'm also involved with the front-end development. I'm familiar with technologies such as the TypeScript based framework Angular, SCSS and LESS, HTML, Javascript, jQuery, PHP as well as Git version control.
