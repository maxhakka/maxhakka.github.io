A Content Management System.

A custom made content management system (CMS) for a healtcare application for iOS and Android.

Background and Problem

A healthcare application for diabetics was in need of a content management system, so that the content could be updated by the client. The application included a carb calculator as well as important information in the form of articles and news.

The home screen in the app, with the latest news and shortcuts to important functions.

The articles in the app have detailed information for diabetics.

Research and Analysis

A number of remote interviews along with two workshops together with the client were conducted in order to establish the needs of the users. A list of requirements were noted down. The requirements inlcuded the ability to add and modify images, articles, news, quizes and checklists.

The information architecture as well as techincal requirements were discussed together with the client and the developers.

Ideation and Design

Based on the research and analysis conducted, a number of designs were created in Adobe XD. Based on testing and feedback, the designs were updated and a number of additional functions were added, such as the ability to upload files.
