A Redesign for an Online Marketplace.

A redesigned version of an online marketplace for second-hand goods for the Swedish market.

Background and Problem

The task involved a fictional case of redesigning an online marketplace for second hand goods. The delivereables included creating wireframes and a new visual concept for the marketplace.

Research and Analysis

Because of the fictional nature of the case, the reseach phase consisted of competitor analysis as well as an analysis of the user groups. The competitor analysis consisted of looking at about a dozen competitor sites and analysing them.

A number of user groups were also identified based on resarch. Young people who were mainly mobile users, were a major demographic that would be taken in to account and catered to.

Ideation and Design

The first part of the design consisted of creating wireframes for a number of important pages. In this case, the pages that were included were the home page and the category page.

The home page included call-to-action buttons, as well as recommended items based on previous searches, popular categories and price drops in order to increase conversion of customers.

Responsive design was also a major consideration, as a site that would be easily adaptable to screens of diffent sizes is important considering the major demographics use mainly their phones to browse the site.
